| **Owner** | Head of Admissions | **Review Date** | October 26, 2024 |

---

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this SOP is to define a standardized, transparent, and efficient process for recruiting, evaluating, and enrolling students. This ensures that all candidates meet the school’s academic standards, align with institutional values, and comply with financial requirements.

## 2. Scope
This procedure applies to all prospective students (Pre-K through Grade 12), their parents/guardians, the Admissions Department, Academic Heads, the Finance Office, and the Registrar.

## 3. Roles and Responsibilities
*   **Admissions Officer:** Manages leads, schedules tours, and maintains the Student Information System (SIS).
*   **Administrative Staff:** Verifies documentation and eligibility.
*   **Academic Department:** Proctors and grades entrance assessments.
*   **Principal/Head of Admissions:** Conducts interviews and evaluates cultural fit.
*   **Admissions Committee:** Makes final selection based on capacity and merit.
*   **Finance Office:** Processes payments and verifies contract execution.
*   **Registrar:** Finalizes enrollment and assigns student records.

---

## 4. Step-by-Step Procedure

### 4.1 Inquiry and Lead Management
*   **Owner:** Admissions Officer
*   **Action:** Upon receipt of an inquiry, the officer must provide the school prospectus and fee structure.
*   **Measurable Threshold:** 
    *   Initial response must be sent within **24 business hours** of inquiry.
    *   Lead must be logged in the SIS with "Inquired" status and 100% of contact fields completed.
*   **Decision:** If the parent declines a tour or further contact, archive the lead within 48 hours.

### 4.2 Application Submission and Document Verification
*   **Owner:** Administrative Staff
*   **Action:** Review the submitted application package for completeness and age eligibility.
*   **Measurable Threshold:** 
    *   Verification must be completed within **2 business days** of submission.
    *   **Pass/Fail Criteria:** Application is "Valid" only if it contains: (1) Birth Certificate, (2) Last 2 years of transcripts, (3) Signed application form, (4) Confirmed receipt of non-refundable application fee.
*   **Decision:** If incomplete, notify the parent via email. The application remains "Pending" for a maximum of 10 business days before being closed.

### 4.3 Entrance Assessment (Academic Evaluation)
*   **Owner:** Academic Department
*   **Action:** Administer standardized Math, English, and Logic tests.
*   **Measurable Threshold:** 
    *   Assessments must be graded and uploaded to the SIS within **3 business days** of the test date.
    *   **Passing Threshold:** A minimum aggregate score of **70%** is required to proceed to the interview stage.
*   **Decision:** If the score is <70%, issue a formal rejection letter within 24 hours of grading.

### 4.4 Admissions Interview (Cultural & Behavioral Fit)
*   **Owner:** Principal / Head of Admissions
*   **Action:** Conduct a formal interview with the student and parents using the *Standardized Interview Rubric*.
*   **Measurable Threshold:** 
    *   Interview must be scheduled within **5 business days** of passing the academic assessment.
    *   **Passing Threshold:** A minimum score of **15 out of 25** on the rubric (covering values alignment, behavior, and language proficiency).
*   **Decision:** Candidates scoring <15 are rejected or placed on a "Hold" status for secondary review.

### 4.5 Final Admission Decision & Offer
*   **Owner:** Admissions Committee
*   **Action:** Review the consolidated file against current seat capacity per grade level.
*   **Measurable Threshold:** 
    *   Final decision must be communicated within **48 hours** of the interview.
    *   **Capacity Check:** Offer can only be issued if `Current Enrolled + Pending Offers < Grade Capacity`.
*   **Decision:** If the grade is at 100% capacity but the candidate passed all criteria, move the status to "Waitlist."

### 4.6 Fee Payment and Contract Execution
*   **Owner:** Finance Office / Parent
*   **Action:** Issue the Offer Letter, Enrollment Contract, and Invoice.
*   **Measurable Threshold:** 
    *   The parent must sign the contract and pay the admission fee within **5 business days** of the offer date.
*   **Decision:** If the deadline passes without payment or a written extension request, the offer is automatically rescinded in the SIS, and the seat is offered to the first candidate on the Waitlist.

### 4.7 Enrollment Confirmation and Onboarding
*   **Owner:** Registrar
*   **Action:** Assign Student ID, allocate class section, and issue the Welcome Pack.
*   **Measurable Threshold:** 
    *   SIS status must be updated to "Enrolled" within **24 hours** of payment verification.
    *   Welcome Pack (Digital) must be sent within **1 business day** of enrollment.
*   **Output:** Official Enrollment Letter and student credentials.

---

## 5. Exceptions and Contingencies

| **Scenario** | **Action** | **Authorization** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Missing Transcripts** | If the previous school withholds records, a "Conditional Offer" may be issued pending receipt within 30 days. | Head of Admissions |
| **Borderline Test Scores** | If a student scores 65-69%, a re-test or a "Probationary Admission" may be granted. | Principal |
| **Sibling Priority** | Siblings of current students move to the top of the Waitlist automatically. | Admissions Officer |
| **Late Payment** | A one-time 3-day extension may be granted upon written request. | Finance Manager |

---

## 6. Documentation and Records
*   All assessment scores and interview rubrics must be stored digitally in the SIS for a minimum of 5 years.
*   Signed Enrollment Contracts must be stored in both digital (PDF) and physical formats.
